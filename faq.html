Frequestly Asked Questions

01. How many HDD projects have Enikkom group completed successfully?

Over 100km and counting. We have done Pipeline installations by HDD in various terrains

02. What is the longest single drill installation done by ECL by HDD

We have done a 3.3km single drill for our clients, such as the Nigeria National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC)

03. How deep can you install a pipeline by HDD?

Our designs can go further than 50m below the ground. We carry out suitable soil investigations to determine proposed depth for all our crossings

04. How long does it take to install a pipeline by HDD?

This is dependent of the length of crossing - different lengths with different installation duration

05. What are the risk associated with HDD?

There are various associated risks:

A) Environmental factors as a result of different terrain types but with our sophisticated soil investigation procedures we are able to go around this

B) Security factors: we go into the volatile places and we install / replace pipelines

C) Equipment & tool failure: we have specialized personnel handling our equipment to ensure they are always in good and safe working conditions by HSE standards. Our tooling selection procedure is also top notch that enables us pick best tools for each job as it comes with its peculiarities, etc.
